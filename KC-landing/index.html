Improves KUKA Robot Utilization

KUKA Connect turns your KUKA robot data into intelligent information to help you utilize your assets more efficiently. Big data, live and historic, is transformed and visualized to fuel better decision making for your overall operations.

Reduces Unplanned Downtime

KUKA Connect automates the maintenance of your KUKA robots by scheduling current and future maintenance tasks and alerts you of possible events that may cause unplanned downtime.

Enables Collaborative Ecosystem

KUKA Connect’s infrastructure enables a collaborative ecosystem where third parties can fuse robot-centric data with process data to create more tailored and vertical solutions for specific applications.

Insight at anytime

KUKA Connect’s responsive interface allows users to securely view and share their connected robots’ data in seconds, any time and from any device.
